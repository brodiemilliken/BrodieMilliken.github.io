I’m Brodie Milliken, a future-driven computer science major at the Ohio State University with a love for physics and simulation design.

I am specializing in software and game development and also minoring in physics. My favorite part about being in computer science is the freedom for creativity that it opens up. Combining physics and coding to create simulations has been a dream of mine since I first wrote down “hello world” on my first day. Now that I have reached a point in my life where I can begin to start making these simulations. I want to share them. Anything from vector fields to simulate wind, to simulations of tens of thousands of asteroids orbiting a planet all interacting with each other. I hope to work in a field someday where I can apply the skills that I have been learning.
